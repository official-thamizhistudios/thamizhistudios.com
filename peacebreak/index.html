🧠 What Is PeaceBreak?

PeaceBreak is a minimalist ambient audio application that lets you trigger intentional moments of calm in the background — without opening the app or disrupting your workflow. It’s mindfulness without the burden of mindfulness apps.

✨ Key Features

🎧 High-Quality Playback

Looping nature sounds, fade-in/fade-out, background and lock screen controls.

⏱ Scheduled Peace Breaks

Recurring or one-off breaks with customizable duration and sounds.

🤳 Shake-to-Trigger

Even when locked — just shake to activate instant calm. No tap needed.
