At Thamizhi Studios, we want you to be completely satisfied with PeaceBreak. This Refund Policy explains our approach to refunds, cancellations, and billing practices for our subscription service.

1. Free Trial Period

  • All new users receive a 7-day free trial
  • No charges during the trial period
  • Cancel anytime during the trial at no cost
  • Full access to premium features during trial
  • Trial automatically converts to paid subscription unless canceled
  • Only one free trial per user account

2. Subscription Billing

  • Monthly subscription: $4.00 USD per month
  • Billing occurs on the same date each month
  • Payments processed through your device's app store
  • Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled
  • Price includes all applicable taxes where required
  • Payment methods accepted via App Store/Google Play

3. Cancellation Policy

Easy Cancellation:

  • Cancel your subscription anytime through the app or device settings
  • No cancellation fees or penalties
  • Access continues until the end of your current billing period
  • Cancellation takes effect at the next renewal date
  • You can reactivate your subscription at any time
  • Account data is retained for 30 days after cancellation

4. Refund Eligibility

Full Refunds Available For:

  • Technical issues preventing app usage for more than 7 days
  • Billing errors or unauthorized charges
  • Service not delivered as promised
  • Duplicate charges or payment processing errors
  • Account security breaches affecting billing

Partial Refunds Available For:

  • Service interruptions lasting more than 48 hours
  • Significant feature removal without notice
  • Data loss due to our technical failures
  • Accessibility issues not resolved within 7 days

5. Refund Limitations

Please Note:

  • No refunds for change of mind after trial period
  • No partial refunds for unused portions of monthly subscriptions
  • No refunds for subscription periods older than 60 days
  • No refunds if account was terminated for terms violation
  • No refunds for content you've already consumed
  • Maximum one refund per account per year

6. How to Request a Refund

Refund Process:

  1. Contact our support team within 30 days of the charge
  2. Provide your account email and order details
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Include any relevant screenshots or documentation
  5. Our team will review and respond within 5 business days
  6. Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days

7. App Store Refunds

  • Payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play
  • You may request refunds directly through your app store
  • App store refund policies also apply
  • We honor valid app store refund decisions
  • Contact us if app store denies your refund request
  • Some refunds require coordination between us and the app store

8. Refund Processing

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Bank processing may take additional 1-3 business days
  • Email confirmation sent when refund is processed
  • Contact us if you don't receive confirmation within 14 days
  • Currency conversions use current exchange rates

9. Service Credits

Alternative to Refunds:

  • Credits extend your subscription at no cost
  • Applied automatically to your next billing cycle
  • Non-transferable and expire if account is closed
  • Cannot be converted to cash or refunds
  • Issued at our discretion for service interruptions

10. Fair Usage

Anti-Abuse Measures:

  • We monitor for unusual refund patterns
  • Accounts with excessive refund requests may be flagged
  • Fraudulent refund attempts will result in account termination
  • We reserve the right to deny refunds for abuse
  • Legal action may be taken for payment fraud

  • Support: support@thamizhistudios.com
  • Billing: billing@thamizhistudios.com
